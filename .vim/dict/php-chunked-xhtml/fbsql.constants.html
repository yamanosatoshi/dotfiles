定義済み定数

FBSQL_ISO_READ_COMMITTED (int)
FBSQL_ISO_REPEATABLE_READ (int)
FBSQL_ISO_SERIALIZABLE (int)
FBSQL_ISO_VERSIONED (int)
FBSQL_UNKNOWN (int)

FBSQL_STARTING (int)
